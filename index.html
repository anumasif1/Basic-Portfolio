About Me

My name is Anum. I moved from Pakistan to California in 2015 and since then, I have lived in Irvine as a homemaker. I am 27 years old, have a 2 year old son "Haziq" and a supporting husband "Farjad". I am currently a student of Coding Bootcamp at University of California Irvine. Web development and programming is new to me. It's challenging at whole lot level but, this is something I enjoy doing. I completed my graduate studies in Anthropology and I have worked for non-profit/multi-national organizations for over 2 years. I have always been an extrovert and enjoyed working with diverse teams. Although, my professional experience and educational background is different from my current career path, I believe that the soft and hard skills I have learnt will be a huge add on to my work.
